About

Cole Calfee is a Southern artist working across fine art, writing, and creative direction. His practice explores presence and meaning through painting, drawing, long-form essays, and narrative projects.

His work is shaped by attention—how it is formed by place, technology, and the stories we live inside. He favors clarity over noise and continuity over trend.

Founder of Haven. Based in the American South.
